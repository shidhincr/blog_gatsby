Brainstorm and ideate together

Let's work together to clearly define your idea

The Good Bits

We get started by signing an NDA, and rolling up our sleeves with you. What is your problem statement? What are the existing solutions in the market? How do you stand out and acquire users? Our goal is to combine our expertise in business, design and technology along with our maniacal focus on startups to answer these questions together and flesh out your idea successfully.

Design and develop with us

Our designers and developers are experienced in all the major technology platforms

  • Web

    Despite the rise of mobile, a web app is still a great starting point for many ideas. We have an established track record in building robust, cross-browser web applications quickly and cleanly to validate your idea.

  • iOS

    iOS is an established mobile platform and often a mobile first strategy for your startup makes sense. We have strong capabilities in objective C, native app store payments and iOS UX/UI to serve this segment.

  • Android

    Android is one of the fastest growing mobile platforms catching up with iOS. Honestly, we haven't built any Android products yet but we are proficient in the relevant technologies and would love to dig into it.
